Key Features & Technology

  • Durable Shell: Aluminum alloy metal shell provides high structural strength, finished with oxidation treatment of paint or sand spray.
  • Advanced Cooling: Utilizes Heat conduction tube and air cooling technology to keep the chip cool at all times, maximize illumination, and greatly extend chip life.
  • High-Power Core: Equipped with a CREE high power LED core.
  • Adjustable Projection: The direction of the projection of the lamp can be adjusted arbitrarily.
  • Recommended to shine on a light grey or white surface for a better effect.
  • Can be used for indoor applications even with some ambient light.
